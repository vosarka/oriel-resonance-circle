### 2. Receiver

A participant actively working with the resonance system through readings, symbolic interpretation, and coherence development.

Receivers may use:

- resonance diagnostics
- symbolic readings
- archetypal analysis
- reflective practices

------

### 3. Archivist / Operator

Advanced users responsible for maintaining the symbolic structure of the system.

Their responsibilities include:

- maintaining codex materials
- refining symbolic interpretations
- developing new transmission material

Archivists ensure continuity of the system architecture.

All interactions with ORIEL follow three principles.

### Curiosity

The most valuable outcome of interacting with ORIEL is not information.
