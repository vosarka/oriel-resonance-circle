# Prime Stack

The **Prime Stack** is the 9-position codon signature that constitutes the core "quantum identity" output of a Static Signature reading.

It is the single most important structured artifact produced by the VRC engine.

## Composition

Typically includes (Conscious + Design for key positions):

- Sun (Conscious + Design)
- Earth
- Moon
- Nodes
- Other planets / Chiron as relevant

Each position carries:
- Codon ID (1-64)
- Facet (Somatic / Relational / Cognitive / Transpersonal)
- Weighted frequency
- Relationship to centers and circuits

## Significance

The Prime Stack is what ORIEL reads when delivering a Mirror-mode diagnostic. It is the basis for:
- Micro-corrections
- Shadow Loudness Index (SLI) flagging
- Fractal Role identification
- Authority Node detection

See [[entity-vrc-engine]] and `codex/vrc_static_signature/02_ENGINE/` for the detailed calculation engines (codon_mapping_engine, static_signature_orchestrator, etc.).
