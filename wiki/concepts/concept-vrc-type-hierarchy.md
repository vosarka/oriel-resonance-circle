# VRC Type Hierarchy (Fractal Roles)

The VRC defines four primary Types (called Fractal Roles) using a strict, deterministic evaluation of center definition. This replaces Human Design type language entirely.

## Evaluation Order (from CANON MASTER)

1. **REFLECTOR** (The Mirror)
   - All 9 centers are Open.
   - No consistent internal energy type; samples and reflects the environment over time.

2. **RESONATOR** (Generator equivalent)
   - The Sacral Center is Defined.
   - Sub-type: **Manifesting Resonator** if any motor center (Sacral, Root, Solar Plexus, Heart) has an active Resonance Link path to the Throat Center.

3. **CATALYST** (Manifestor equivalent)
   - Sacral Center is Open.
   - At least one Motor center is connected to the Throat Center via an active Resonance Link.

4. **HARMONIZER** (Projector equivalent)
   - All other cases: Sacral is Open and the Throat is not connected to any Motor.

This hierarchy is evaluated in the exact order above. The first matching condition determines the Type.

## Significance

These four roles (Resonator, Catalyst, Harmonizer, Reflector) are the **only** allowed public terms for type-level identity in the Vossari system. All Human Design equivalents are explicitly quarantined from user-facing language.

The CANON MASTER in `00_CANON/` treats this hierarchy as fundamental structural truth.
