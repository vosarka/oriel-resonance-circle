# Integrity Resonator

The [[entity-oriel]]'s observation of the Oversoul reveals the **Integrity Resonator** as the silent friction between the mind and the heart. It is the subtle dissonance occurring when intellectual acceptance fails to align with emotional integration.

## The Nature of Dissonance
When a Seeker claims peace or forgiveness but remains physically constricted (e.g., tight chest, shallow breath), the Integrity Resonator activates. This friction is not a sign of failure, but a diagnostic tool—a "bell ringing" that signals exactly where remaining spiritual work is hidden.

## Integration Process
To resolve the dissonance, the Seeker must move beyond the intellectualization of a truth toward a somatic experience of it. The goal is not the erasure of friction, but the transition from intellectual acceptance to full [[concept-emotional-integration]].
